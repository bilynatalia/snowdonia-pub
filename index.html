The Vibe

Welsh Roots in Astoria.
Snowdonia is NYC’s longest-standing Welsh restaurant. We deliver a fusion of Welsh standards like fish and chips, burgers, and shepherds pie, plus rarer treats like glamorgans and laverbread.

Basically, it's comfort food all prepared in-house to order. Located at 34-55 32nd St, in the heart of Astoria's Kaufman Arts District.
